Mondesk Authentication

Due to Monzo's OAuth2 system, we need to jump through a few hoops to continue. Never fear, this tool automates a lot of it for you!

To continue, you will want to sign into the Monzo developer portal in another tab. From here, click Clients at the top and then New OAuth Client. Then you will want to change Confidentiality to Confidential and set the redirect URL as EXACTLY https://mondesk-auth.jakegealer.me/. The name can be anything.
